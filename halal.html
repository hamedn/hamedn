NYC Halal Restaurant Map

Above is an index of all of the halal restaurants in NYC that I've discovered during my first year of college. See the notes below for more information. Please feel free to submit any feedback or restaurant additions using this form.

1. I strongly recommend that you call restaurants to confirm that they are halal. I have made my best effort to include restaurants that I am confident in, but due to the number of restaurants there may always be errors.

2. I have not included some locations in this map, notably fried-chicken or halal-cart type places. Moreover, there is a clear Manhattan and Brooklyn bias. If you see many restaurants missing, please fill out the above form and I will happily add them to the list.

3. Most partial halal locations are non-Muslim establishments with known Halaal meat suppliers. (Creekstone, Madani Halal, etc.) Typically these are higher-end establishments with menus that are more subject to change, so please call and confirm before eating there.

4. This listing would not be possible without data from Hadi Nilforoshan as well as Zabiha.com

5. I have not been to every restaurant on this list. However, I would specifically recommend the following places: Nur Thai, ABA Turkish Restaurant, Mighty Quinns Barbecue (Creekstone Brisket, Burnt Ends, Brontasaurus Rib, Dirty Fries), Africa Kine, Safari, Honest Chops Burgers, Munch, Cafe Cluny (Creekstone Burger)
